Gigi Hadid Pasta

Ingredients needed:

  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cream
  • Vodka
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Parsely and Basil

Steps:

  1. Make the Sauce:

    2. Sweat the aromatics in hot olive oil in a large skillet, add tomato paste, and cook until it caramelizes. To that, add cream and vodka. Once the alcohol evaporates, season with chili flakes, salt, and pepper.

  2. Cook Pasta:

    3. In a large pot, cook pasta according to the instructions, and save some water before draining it.

  3. Combine The Pasta:

    4. Add butter to the sauce as it cooks, and let it simmer on medium heat. Add the cooked pasta, cooking water, and parmesan, and cook until it all combines.

  4. Garnish and serve:

    5. Finish the pasta with freshly chopped parsley and basil and serve hot.
